# Code of Conduct — Team Johannes Gutenberg

## Introduction
As members of **Team Johannes Gutenberg**, we commit to maintaining a positive, safe, and productive environment during all our activities, including meetings, workshops, events, and competitions such as Eurobot Junior.

This Code of Conduct outlines the expectations for team members, mentors, partners, and collaborators.

---

## Our Principles
### 1. Respect
- Treat all team members, mentors, competitors, and organizers with respect.
- Listen actively to others’ ideas and viewpoints.
- Resolve conflicts calmly and constructively.

### 2. Integrity
- Compete fairly and honestly.
- Do not plagiarize or claim ownership of others’ work.
- Follow the rules of Eurobot Junior and any additional national regulations.

### 3. Inclusion
- Ensure everyone feels welcome and valued.
- Avoid any form of discrimination or exclusion.
- Encourage participation from all members regardless of skill level.

### 4. Collaboration
- Share knowledge openly within the team.
- Support teammates who need help.
- Celebrate successes together and learn from setbacks.

### 5. Safety
- Follow all safety instructions when working with tools, electronics, or equipment.
- Immediately report unsafe behavior or hazards.
- Keep the workspace clean and organized.

---

## Unacceptable Behavior
The following actions are not tolerated:
- Harassment, bullying, or intimidation  
- Destructive criticism or mocking others’ ideas  
- Reckless or unsafe behavior  
- Disrupting meetings, workshops, or competitions  
- Ignoring competition rules or mentor guidance  

---

## Reporting Issues
If you observe behavior that violates this Code of Conduct:
- Speak directly to a mentor  
- Or, contact the team coordinator privately  

All reports will be handled confidentially and respectfully.

---

## Commitment
By contributing to this project or participating in team activities, you agree to uphold this Code of Conduct.

**Team Johannes Gutenberg — Building robots with creativity, respect, and engineering excellence.**
